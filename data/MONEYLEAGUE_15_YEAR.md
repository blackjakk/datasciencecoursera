# 🏆 MONEYLEAGUE — 15 Years
*May 2026 · 2011 through 2025 · the chronological story*

---

## How we got here

This league started in **2011 with 8 teams** on Yahoo, expanded to 10 in 2013, expanded again to 12 in 2019, and migrated to Sleeper in 2023. The original 8-team roster has been mostly weeded out — only a handful of the founders are still here.

**Founders still here (since 2011):** Brian, Coop, Eric, Kyle, Lem, Trevor
