Grading v2: rest-of-season points ABOVE POSITIONAL REPLACEMENT (PAR) swung — each side's received-minus-sent PAR over the weeks AFTER the trade week through week 17. A player-week's PAR = his Sleeper pts_half_ppr (fallback pts_ppr − 0.5×rec) minus a fixed per-position weekly replacement level; weeks without a stat line count 0 (a replacement body starts instead). Replacement level = that season's median weekly score of the rank-N player at the position, N = QB12 / RB30 / WR36 / TE12 / K12 / DEF12 (the last body a 12-team room starts anyway); computed levels — 2023: QB 18.66 / RB 7.2 / WR 8.5 / TE 8.4 / K 9 / DEF 9 · 2024: QB 19.36 / RB 8.2 / WR 8.9 / TE 8.4 / K 9 / DEF 8 · 2025: QB 19.72 / RB 8.1 / WR 8.3 / TE 9.3 / K 10 / DEF 7. PAR keeps QB volume from buying every verdict: raw half-PPR points ride along in the raw column for reference, and rank shifts vs the raw table are flagged in the # column. Draft picks and FAAB are listed on the ledger but graded 0: a pick's value realizes at a future draft and grading it here would be guesswork dressed as arithmetic. Picks-only deals are marked PUSH, not wins. W–L–P counts a win when a side's PAR swing is positive. Verdict names the side that gained more PAR; it is hindsight, not intent.
