Fair questions

Will it work in the draft room on my phone?
Yes — built phone-first at 360px: the columns that matter (position, name, value, price, survival) stay on screen, the clock stays pinned, and touch targets are draft-day-thumb sized.
What if the wifi dies mid-draft?
Grab standalone.html from the helper directory beforehand — the entire terminal with all data inlined in one file. It runs from a saved file with zero network.
Where do the odds actually come from?
Each week the pipeline pulls fresh projections and market prices, then plays out 300 full drafts against bots modeled on each manager's real historical tendencies (yes, including the early kicker). Survival % is where each player left the board across those 300 runs.
Does it draft for me?
No — it prices the board; you make the call. The backtest section above is honest about why: the edge isn't picking players for you, it's knowing the probabilities and contract costs your gut can't compute at pick 6 with ninety seconds on the clock.
Why "GUAP"?
House currency. Projections are denominated, keepers are bonds, and the fine print is legally binding in spirit only.
